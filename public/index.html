Elephant Chart is an organization application. It features a skewmorphic (life-like) drag and drop, and saveable custom charts and layouts. Music Experiment

Music Trainer & Generator

on-going development
basic UI


Tech

  • ES6
  • WebAudio
  • Local Storage

JavaScript ES6 and the WebAudio API are used to provide a web-based ear-trainer and infinite music generator. The tools help a student train their musical ear in the subjects of melody, relative pitch, pitch memory, and tempo. The Music Generator is a passion project and experiment that I will return to. My more recent study in object-oriented principles and software architecture has led me to realize the entirety of the project needs refactor and rearchitecture! However, the underlying logic in how to solve the 'musical problems' works well.

The Music Generator is the conclusion to a hypothesis I formed while teaching music. My theory was that if you programmed all the 'rules' of music theory and then allowed for randomization in it's choices, the results would be surprisingly familiar and even sound pretty good at times- with only occasional awkward moments! The goal of the project, given further development, is that the generator will express the power of Western Musical structures (usually termed 'music theory') by allowing them to 'speak' without human interaction, showcasing their merit and the 'natural behavior' of music.

The application has a high level of educational value for any musician. The Melodic Trainer facilitates musical development through use of an invaluable skill that is difficult to methodically practice. The melodic idea consisting of the same note, but different tones/instruments, is called "klangfarbenmelodie". The trainer utilizes and teaches this with a mode randomizing the playback to multiple orchestral instrument choices. This develops the ear to be sensitive to pitch while listening to instruments outside of the student's familiarity, accentuating the 'pitch' side of the learning.

Full-stack Template

LumberJack Theme

in-production


Tech

  • Node.js
  • Express
  • EJS
  • Prismic
  • Curator
  • NodeMailer

"LumberJack" is a Full Stack template developed for small businesses and social media creators. The theme integrates third-party services to eliminate update, security, and issues associated with code-heavy CMS templates. Featuring a headless CMS with Prismic, a multi-network aggregator in Curator, and automatic email responses by NodeMailer, LumberJack's needs for maintenance and updates are low. Ships with detailed setup instructions.

Making use of third-party API's keeps the website operator from having to engage with the site code after launch, keeping user-breakage to a minimum. LumberJack's blog is generated using EJS and Prismic. Curator allows owners and creators to build feeds from more than one network, enabling more cross-network audience engagement.

Designed for a two-color brand scheme with highly legible greyscale copy. Card elements are modular and can be used in a variety of configurations, color and size. LumberJack is fully compatible with any device.

Creativity and logic are not mutually exclusive, I think they go hand in hand. Organization allows us to communicate effectively and with style!
