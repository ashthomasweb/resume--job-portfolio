JavaScript Program

Music Trainer & Generator
on-going development


Tech

ES6, WebAudio

This project is the conclusion to a hypothesis I had while a music teacher. At that time I did not know anything about programming other than it must follow standard logic principles. In short, I theorized that if you put all the 'rules' of music theory into code and allowed for randomization in it's choices, the resultant music would be surprisingly familiar and even sound pretty good at times- with only occasional awkward moments. This project is two-fold, containing a set of practice tools, as well as a music generator. The tools help a student train their ear in the subjects of melody, relative pitch, pitch memory, and tempo. The music generator is a passion project that I will continue to build upon for some time. In time it will not only be an expression of the power of western musical structures, but also a learning device.

Work your klangfarbenmelodie chops with multiple orchestral instrument choices. The music trainer includes a function which allows for different instruments to randomly substitute the primary instrument sound, while still asking the student to repeat the pattern back. For instance, if you play piano, you can select french horn sounds to work with instead. This develops the ear to be sensitive to pitch while listening to instruments outside of their familiarity. The melodic idea consisting of the same note, but different tones/instruments, is called "klangfarbenmelodie". This type of exercise was extremely beneficial to my own ear during my development, and I had a difficult time finding ways to practice this type of hearing. This aural skill proved to be one of the most powerful connection points in the development of my musical ear. This melody trainer facilitates the study of an invaluable skill that is difficult to methodically practice.





Full-stack Template

LumberJack


Tech

Node.js, Express, EJS, Prismic, Curator, NodeMailer

"LumberJack" is a full-stack template developed for small businesses and social media creators. The theme is lightweight and integrates third-party services to eliminate update, security, and code-heavy issues. Featuring a headless CMS with Prismic, a multi-network aggregator in Curator, and automatic email responses by NodeMailer, LumberJack's needs for maintenance and updates are low. Ships with detailed setup instructions accessible by any front-end developer or diligent person.

Making use of third-party API's keeps the website owner from having to engage with the site code after launch, keeping breakage to a minimum. LumberJack's blog is automatically generated using EJS and comes with a "Custom Type" ready to upload to your Prismic repository. Curator allows owners and creators to build feeds from more than one network, enabling more cross-network audience engagement.

Designed for a two-color brand scheme with highly legible greyscale copy. Card elements are modular and can be used in a variety of configurations, color and size. LumberJack is fully compatible with any device.
